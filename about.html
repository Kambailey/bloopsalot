Hello! I'm Katie!

I also go by Bloopsalot online!

I'm an artist that specializes in illustrations and comics. I have a Bachelor's in Sequential Art from the Savannah College of Art and Design, and am currently working on a Digital Media Associate's degree at Polk State. My passion project has been Haunted Housemate, a vertical scrolling web comic that's been publishing for over three years.

I love writing in a YA and New Adult genre, particularly about love, whether it's love of the self, love of the supernatural, or love of community and nature.

Any Hobbies?

My current interests outside of work are DnD, Minecraft, and Genshin Impact. I've also been playing some Horror Visual Novels on the side, but most of my time is swallowed by school and art!
