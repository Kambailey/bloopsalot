Haunted Housemate

A Veritcal Scrolling Webcomic, telling the story of a man who should be dead meeting a girl who is. They learn they are deserving of love despite their traumas and grow together as people.

Konstantin is a veteran who can barely remember his past. All he knows is that he deserves the scars on his face and wrists but he can't pinpoint why. Kaytlyn is a gentle soul who died in 1963, and is reluctant to tell why, despite her violent outbursts. Over time, they may reveal the truth, or discover it themselves. However, first thing's first, someone needs to make breakfast.
